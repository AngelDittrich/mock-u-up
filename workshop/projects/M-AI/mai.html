M.AI — Emotional Smart Mirror

Product Design, IoT & AI • 2025

M.AI is an intelligent mirror designed to help people understand their emotions effortlessly. By combining artificial intelligence, computer vision, and thoughtful interface design, this project transforms a daily habit: looking in the mirror. Into a moment of emotional awareness.

The Challenge

Emotional well-being apps are more popular than ever, yet most users abandon them within weeks. The main reason is simple: they require too much effort. Writing emotions, filling forms, and manually tracking feelings becomes exhausting over time.

The challenge was to create a solution that works naturally, without asking users to change their behavior. The system had to be intuitive, non-intrusive, and accessible to people with no technical background, while still handling sensitive emotional data in a respectful way.

The Solution

M.AI solves this problem by removing friction. The mirror automatically detects the user, analyzes facial expressions, and identifies emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, or neutrality without requiring any manual input.

The system is built using a Raspberry Pi, a wide-angle camera, and AI libraries like DeepFace. The frontend experience was developed using React for the web platform and React Native for the mobile app, ensuring a smooth and consistent experience across devices.

Users can view daily emotional summaries, compare moods over time, and receive personalized psychological tips, all presented in a clean, minimal interface inspired by Apple’s design language.

Design Philosophy

The visual identity of M.AI focuses on clarity, calm, and emotional depth. Dark tones such as black and space gray create a modern and focused environment, while blue, purple, pink, and red accents represent different emotional states.

Every design decision was made to reduce cognitive load and make the technology feel invisible. The goal was not to impress with complexity, but to create trust, comfort, and emotional connection.

Impact & Learning

This project was not only a technical challenge, but also a human one. It required understanding emotions, user behavior, and the importance of simplicity. Through M.AI, we learned how hardware, software, and design come together to create meaningful experiences.

M.AI represents our vision of technology that supports people quietly, integrates into daily life, and encourages self-awareness without pressure.
